Game On!

Gaming has been a huge part of my life for as long as I can remember. Ive played on a wide variety of consoles over the years, from handheld systems like the PSP, Gameboy, Nintendo DS and DSi, to home consoles like the Wii, Xbox, Xbox 360, Xbox One, PS5, PC, and there might be some that I am forgetting. Each system has given me unique experiences and unforgettable memories.

Favorite Game Genres

While I enjoy many types of games, I usually gravitate toward first-person shooters and sports games. Some of my favorites include:

  • NBA 2K series
  • Madden NFL series
  • Call of Duty (COD) series
  • Fortnite
  • Apex Legends

Monster Hunter Obsession

During quarantine, I had a massive obsession with Monster Hunter: World, spending hours tracking monsters, crafting gear, and exploring new environments. I now play Monster Hunter: Wilds on and off today. The thrill of hunting monsters and strategizing with friends keeps me coming back for more.

Other Gaming Interests

Besides shooters and sports games, I sometimes explore:

  • Adventure and role-playing games (RPGs)
  • Strategy and simulation games
  • Multiplayer co-op games with friends
